Benefits of GOV.UK Pay

GOV.UK Pay make it easier for the public to pay for government services. It also makes it much easier for service teams to process these payments.

Benefits to service teams include:

  • troubleshoot payment queries easily
  • issue refunds and chargebacks
  • reconcile payments
  • generate financial reports for specific time periods
  • send receipt emails to users
  • invite team members with specific permission levels
  • select accepted payment methods and card types

Benefits to public users include:

  • a better and more consistent payment experience across government services
  • a choice regarding how to pay
  • more accessible payment pages
